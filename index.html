Project Kuwait 400

A Strategic Value Engineering Study

Selecting the Optimal Construction System for Speed, Quality, and Profitability.

Vision & Decision Criteria

Project Vision

To establish market leadership by delivering 400 premium villas that set a new benchmark for sustainable construction in Kuwait, offering superior lifetime value to homeowners and maximizing project ROI through rapid execution.

Key Decision Criteria

  1. Total Lifecycle Cost: Beyond initial price to include speed, overhead, and lifetime savings.
  2. Project Velocity: Time-to-market and speed of revenue generation.
  3. Product Quality & Marketability: The final product's appeal and value proposition to buyers.
  4. Risk & Predictability: Minimizing exposure to schedule, cost, and quality risks.

Comparative System Analysis

Option 1: Traditional RC

- Skilled Labor: High dependency

- Foundation: Heavy load, requires large foundations

- On-Site Waste: High (wood, cement bags, block cuts)

- Follow-on Trades: Delayed start due to curing

Option 2: AAC Full Panel

+ Skilled Labor: Low (specialized assembly team)

+ Foundation: Light load, reduced size & cost

+ On-Site Waste: Minimal

+ Follow-on Trades: Immediate start after assembly

Option 3: AAC Mini-Panel

+ Skilled Labor: Low-Medium

+ Foundation: Light load, reduced size & cost

+ On-Site Waste: Very Low

+ Follow-on Trades: Immediate start after assembly

The Value Proposition to the Homebuyer

A better construction method results in a better home. These are powerful, tangible benefits that justify a premium price and drive faster sales.

Lower Energy Bills

"The only building system classified by the Saudi Electricity Company as a structural insulation material." This translates to provable, significant savings for the lifetime of the villa.

Enhanced Safety

A 7-hour fire rating offers unparalleled peace of mind. This is a powerful emotional selling point for families.

Healthier Living

AAC is an inorganic, non-toxic material resistant to mold and pests. The airtight construction also improves indoor air quality.

Quieter Homes

Superior acoustic insulation creates a more serene and private living environment, a luxury feature in modern developments.

Appendix: Financial Assumptions

The following estimates were used for calculating indirect and lifecycle costs:

  • Site Overheads: Estimated at 6,000 SR/month per villa for management, security, and utilities. A 2.5-month schedule saving yields 15,000 SR.
  • Energy Savings: Assumes a conservative 75 SR/month (900 SR/year) saving on electricity bills due to superior insulation. Calculated over a 20-year period, this amounts to 18,000 SR.
  • Waste Removal: Based on typical industry costs for landfill fees and transport for discarded wood formwork, cement bags, and block cuttings.
  • Financing Costs: Not explicitly quantified but acknowledged that a shorter project duration significantly reduces the cost of capital.
  • Market Premium: The potential for a higher sales price due to superior features has not been included, making these projections highly conservative.
